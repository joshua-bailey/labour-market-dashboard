Wages

Average hourly earnings and weekly hours

Average hourly earnings (AHE) is the establishment-survey wage tracker. It is not quality-adjusted — composition shifts can move it independently of any underlying wage pressure — but it is timely. The 3-month annualised view captures recent momentum; the year-on-year view smooths through monthly noise.
