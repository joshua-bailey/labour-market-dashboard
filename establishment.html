Establishment Survey

Monthly change in nonfarm payrolls, by industry

The establishment (CES) survey of payroll employment was not interrupted by the 2025 federal-government shutdown — every series here has continuous monthly data through the latest release. Toggles let you switch between monthly, 3-month, and 6-month moving averages.

Diffusion index

The CES diffusion index is the share of industries adding jobs in a given month (with industries unchanged counted at half-weight). Readings above 50 indicate broad-based hiring; readings below 50 indicate the gains are concentrated.
