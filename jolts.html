JOLTS

Job Openings and Labor Turnover Survey

JOLTS gives the demand side of the labour market: the stock of vacancies and flows through hires, quits, and layoffs. The key tightness reads are the vacancies-to-unemployed (V/U) ratio and the quits rate — workers quit more readily when they have outside options. Both fell back toward their pre-pandemic averages over 2024–25.
